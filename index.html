The workshop "Categorical Logic and Higher Categories" will be held at the University of Manchester from the 16th to the 19th of December 2024. We hope that bringing together researchers with different backgrounds will help to link the development of Higher Category Theory with Categorical Logic so as to inspire new developments, in and beyond Homotopy Type Theory and Univalent Foundations.
